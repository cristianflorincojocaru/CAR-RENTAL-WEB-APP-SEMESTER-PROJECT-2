1. Eligibility

To rent a vehicle with WheelDeal, you must be at least 21 years of age and hold a valid driver's licence that has been active for a minimum of 2 years. Drivers aged 21–24 may be subject to a young-driver surcharge depending on the vehicle category. A valid credit or debit card in the renter's name is required as a security deposit at the time of pick-up.

2. Booking & payment

Reservations are confirmed only upon receipt of full payment or a valid payment guarantee. The price shown at the time of booking is final and includes mandatory insurance and all applicable taxes. Optional add-ons (GPS, child seats, additional driver) are itemised separately. WheelDeal reserves the right to cancel an unconfirmed booking if payment cannot be processed.

3. Cancellation & modification

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before the scheduled pick-up time. Cancellations made within 48 hours of pick-up are subject to a fee equivalent to one day's rental rate. No-shows without prior notice will be charged the full rental amount. Booking modifications are accepted subject to vehicle availability and may affect the total price.

4. Vehicle use

Vehicles must be used solely on paved public roads within Romania unless prior written consent has been obtained. Smoking, transporting animals without protective covers, and any form of commercial transport (taxi, ride-hailing) are strictly prohibited. Off-road use will void all insurance cover. The renter is responsible for any traffic fines or tolls incurred during the rental period.

5. Damage & liability

The renter is liable for all damage to the vehicle occurring during the rental period, up to the excess amount specified in their chosen protection plan. WheelDeal reserves the right to charge the renter's card on file for any damages not covered by the selected plan. A damage report must be completed at pick-up and return. Pre-existing damage documented at pick-up will not be charged.

6. Fuel policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank. Failure to return the vehicle with a full tank will result in a refuelling charge at our current pump rate plus a service fee.

7. Governing law

These Terms of Service are governed by the laws of Romania. Any disputes arising from or relating to these terms shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Bucharest, Romania.
